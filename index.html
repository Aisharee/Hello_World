ABOUT US

Ummu Ibrahim Home Of Hope is an Islamic orphanage center is located in Kasarani constituency Nairobi County at Gitwamba Village in Kenya. This orphanage initiative was founded by sheikh Abdallah Shaaban Hassan and other well wishers who came in to assist him to complete some arrears on buying the plot. We constructed the 1st building in 2005-2006 with a sister in Islam named

Ummu Ibrahim who built this center for the sake of Allah, we later named our home after she passed away later “RahmahaAllah Taala” may Allah reward her good deeds. The Branch for Girls is Located in Matungulu Constituency Machakos County Mutalia Village in Kenya. It was Built by Sheikh Abdallah Shaaban Hassan who is the Founder of Ummu Ibrahim Centre from

2009 to 2011.Up to now our orphanage has 35 boys and 35 girls of whom we got from: the chiefs’ office, village leaders, imams, guardians and others from the streets. The aim of UMMU IBRAHIM HOME OF HOPE is to raise the orphans by emphasizing on Qur’an Memorization and learning of the sunnah of The Prophet Muhammad (Salaallahu Aleihi wassalam)

WHAT WE OFFER

Services

The orphans that we take care of live in our centers and we take care of all their needs from food, clothing, medication, school fees and they learn their delegations (madrassah)in the center where we have Ustadh for boys and Ustadhah for girls.

Income Generating Projects

  • Whole sale

  • Mini Supermarket

  • Restaurant

  • Hospital

  • Farming

Challenges

We are all aware many orphans have no source of income and/or guardians to take care of them, so we cannot charge them for all that we offer them. This is the reason Muslims as a community have to step in and aid them and give them a positive future.
